B2B Paid Search

Rebuilding a National B2B Paid Search Program After Agency Transition

📆 July 2025 – Present 📍 National 🌍 Google Search, Performance Max, Display
10x+ Revenue per lead YoY
50%+ YoY lead growth

The Challenge

Inherited an account heavily reliant on Performance Max (approximately 90% of spend) with branded keywords inflating reported conversions. Leadership had no clear view into what was actually driving non-branded demand. Conversion tracking inconsistencies made month-over-month comparison unreliable.

What I Did

  • Separated branded vs. non-branded search into dedicated campaigns
  • Repositioned Performance Max as a secondary, governed channel
  • Excluded branded keywords from PMax to prevent performance inflation
  • Implemented Dynamic Keyword Insertion and location personalization
  • Standardized UTM parameters across all SEM for GA4 alignment
  • Audited and corrected form submission tracking errors
  • Built a negative keyword list to eliminate wasted spend
  • Initiated Salesforce to Google Ads closed-loop reporting alignment

Paid Search Turnaround

Reactivating a Stalled Account for a National Logistics Provider

📆 November 2024 – Present 🌍 Google Ads 📍 National
+50% Conversions MoM
−20% Cost per acquisition

The Challenge

When the account was inherited in November, ad spend was significantly under target due to restrictive budget settings. Conversions were steadily declining and conversion tracking contained duplicates, including a page view counted as a primary conversion, making performance data unreliable.

What I Did

  • Raised Target CPAs to reduce lost impression share to competitors
  • Switched campaigns to Maximize Clicks to re-activate volume
  • Removed device bid adjustments to let Google optimize naturally
  • Turned off Display Network on Search campaigns
  • Paused high-spend, low-converting keywords
  • Cleaned conversion tracking, removed duplicates and page view primary
  • Added Dynamic Search Ads for broader coverage
  • Implemented keyword and location insertion in ad copy

ABM Strategy & Execution

Full-Funnel ABM Campaign for Government Technology Company Targeting State DOTs Nationally

📆 September 2025 – Present 🌍 LinkedIn, StackAdapt, HubSpot Email 📍 National — State Departments of Transportation
47 DOT accounts reached
3 Channels coordinated

The Challenge

The client offers two distinct products targeting state Departments of Transportation: a Supplier Diversity management platform and a labor compliance and certified payroll reporting solution. Government buyers operate on long procurement cycles, make decisions by committee, and rarely fill out forms early in their journey. The challenge was building a coordinated ABM program that could generate measurable engagement across a highly specific named account list, with no paid search intent to rely on, while surfacing meaningful buying signals for the sales team.

What I Did

  • Designed the full 3-stage campaign strategy: audience building, engagement and nurture, retargeting and conversion
  • Built and maintained the named account target list across state DOT agencies nationally
  • Launched coordinated LinkedIn and StackAdapt ABM campaigns for both B2Gnow and eComply product tracks simultaneously
  • Wrote LinkedIn ad copy, InMail sequences, and 4-email nurture series for each product track
  • Configured HubSpot workflows to enroll contacts after content downloads and trigger sales alerts on warm leads
  • Built client-facing interactive reporting deliverables: account-level engagement tables, cross-platform bar charts, and video completion reports by named DOT account
  • Tracked post-ad intent signals (demo page visits without a click) as the primary buying signal for government accounts
  • Proposed and designed a focused 1:1 content strategy for prioritizing high-value individual DOT targets
